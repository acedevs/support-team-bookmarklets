EMC Custom Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet is a small program that you can store in your bookmark toolbar.

To use this tool, use your mouse to click and drag the following link to your bookmark bar:

Create Cards

Once the link is in your bookmarks, you can click on it when you have an open card onscreen. It will detect the checklists on the open card, and convert all of the items into cards. It will also replace the existing checklist items with links to the new cards.

Provided by your Trello Enterprise team.
